290D Solar Lighting Fixture

ABOUT 290D

The 290D is a “Cobra Head” style light fixture with modern style elements. The 290D fixture is powered by a state of the art, high efficiency, DC LED driver that powers either 24 or 48 CREE LEDs. These 2 LED configurations allow us to supply a wide range of lumen packages ranging from low to high power, making this fixture extremely flexible.
